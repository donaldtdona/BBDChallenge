About the event

The BBD Software x Umuzi.org Immersion Day is a unique opportunity exclusively for Umuzi students, made possible by BBD's generous sponsorship. This event is designed to inspire and motivate you to excel in your studies. On Immersion Day, you'll get an inside look at BBD's dynamic work environment, experiencing firsthand what it would be like to be part of their team. You'll also have the chance to engage with BBD staff and ask questions, including from Umuzi alumni who have walked the same path you're on. It's a day dedicated to your growth, inspiration, and the start of your tech career journey with BBD and Umuzi.



Requirements for the Event

To participate in the BBD Software x Umuzi.org Immersion Day, students must meet the following requirements:

  • You must be a current student at Umuzi studying web development
  • You must be sponsored by BBD Software
  • For students within Johannesburg, physical attendance is compulsory.
  • Students outside Johannesburg may attend remotely or choose to come to the event at their own transport cost.
  • Complete and submit the event application form by the specified deadline.
  • Be prepared to engage actively, ask questions, and participate in discussions with BBD staff and Umuzi alumni.
